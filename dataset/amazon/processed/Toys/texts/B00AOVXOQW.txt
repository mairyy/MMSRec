Title: Brio Battery Operated Action Train ; Description: Founded in Sweden in 1884, Brio makes the highest quality wooden toys that have delighted children and created happy memories for generations. Both educational and fun, Brio's uncompromising focus on design, quality and craftsmanship create toys of lasting value that stimulate creativity and imagination. This is one hard working train and has the action to match. The Brio Battery Operated Action Train has a motorized engine and hauls a cement mixer car and a coal wagon. Press the button on the engine cab and the train comes to life and moves down the track. The illuminated headlight shines the way. The cement mixer spins and the coal wagon rocks back and forth. The Brio Battery Operated Action Train, as with everything in the Brio system is engineered to work together for endless expansion and creative play options. ;
