Title: Thomas the Train: Follow Me Thomas ; Description: Now every child can pretend to be a train engineer and lead Thomas on his way. Follow Me Thomas includes a preschool sized Thomas engine and a light up lantern. The child can point and move the light and Thomas will follow along. As he moves, he will say interactive phrases and sound effects. When you press any of the buttons on the lantern, Thomas will do one of three programmed moves including, figure 8&#x2019;s, circles, and zig zags.
