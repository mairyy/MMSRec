Title: Fisher-Price Team Umizoomi: UmiPhone ; Description: Learn numbers, patterns and colors by making and receiving calls to or from your favorite Team Umizoomi characters. Press buttons 0-9 on the keypad to identify the numbers, or dial Milli, Geo or Bot directly by dialing 1-2-3, 4-5-6 or 7-8-9. Press the message button to pick up messages from Milli, Geo or Bot and answer their questions. The Umi Counting Phone also has an Umi Car button which you can press to hear the Umi Car song.
