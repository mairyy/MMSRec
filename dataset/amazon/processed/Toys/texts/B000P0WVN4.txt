Title: Diggity Dog ; Description: Adorable first memory board game for kids.  Help the puppies find their bones.  Press on the electronic barking dog and he will bark the number of spaces you move - 1, 2 or 3.  When your puppy lands on a space beside a bone, he can pick it up using his magnetic nose.  If the color of the bone matches your puppy, you get to move it to your doghouse.  The first to find 3 matching bones wins.  Contents:  game board, 4 puppies, 1 electronic barking dog, 12 bones, game rules.  1-4 players.  Ages 3+. ;
