Title: PLAYMOBIL My Take Along 1.2.3 Noahs Ark ; Description: Hurry to help the animals board the My Take Along 1.2.3 Noah's Ark. The set's colorful, larger, rounded pieces are specially designed for toddlers. The deck of the ark is removable and the hull has plenty of space for storage. The set comes with a handle so it can be taken anywhere and the ark can float on water. Set includes two figures, lion and lioness and pairs of giraffes, zebras, elephants and birds. Dimensions: 12.2 x 7.1 x 7.1 Inch (L x W x H). Recommended for ages 18 months and up. Playmobil, Germany's largest toy manufacturer, is headquartered in Zirndorf, Germany and has been producing and distributing toys for more than 36 years. The miniature worlds of Playmobil encourage children to explore and learn while having fun. Children's imaginations are transported to medieval times with the Playmobil castle, to the adventurous seas with Playmobil pirates and many other themed playworlds.
