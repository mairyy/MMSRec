Title: Angry Birds: On Thin Ice Game ; Description: Angry Birds: On Thin Ice Game: Save the Angry Birds. The second installment of the exciting skill and action game based on the best-selling phone app, Angry Birds On Thin Ice, lets players build, launch and destroy with fun new features, including pigs with helmets, breakable ice block building pieces, an exploding TNT box and a new large red bird character. Players draw a card and build the structure shown before their opponents launch Angry Birds in an attempt to knock down the structure-and the pigs-to win points. Includes 2 Angry Birds, 3 green pigs, 1 slingshot-style catapult launcher, structure pieces and mission cards. For 2-4 players. ;
