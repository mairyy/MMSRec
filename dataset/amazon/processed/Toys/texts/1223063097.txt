Title: Rack-O ; Description: Since the "Fabulous 50's", families have enjoyed this Rack'em up fun-filled card game. This new addition, featuring fine quality card with bold numbers, will delight players young and old. Rack-O is timeless fun. Players randomly slot 10 cards on their rack. The race then begins to draw and discard until one player positions 10 cards in numberical sequence. Sound simple? It is - but you will need skillful play, concentration and just a little luck to win. ;
