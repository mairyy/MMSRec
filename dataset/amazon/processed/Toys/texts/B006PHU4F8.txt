Title: Playmobil Take Along Soccer Match ; Description: Playmobil Take Along Soccer Match: Dribble your way across the field in the Take Along Soccer Match. With six athlete figures primed and ready to play, this set comes fully equipped with everything kids need to have a complete game experience. This compactable and travel-ready set also includes a 22 x 33 inch field, soccer ball and other accessories.
