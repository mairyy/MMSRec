Title: ThinkFun Snack Attack ; Description: Craving a fun time. Bring Snack Attack. to the table and serve up a fast-paced picture matching game. Start play by cranking the Spinner Platter to reveal seven Snack Tokens, then race to match these tokens to the foods already on your plate. You win by collecting the most Snack Tokens. Snack Attack builds memory and concentration skills in younger players and is entertaining fun for the whole family. ;
