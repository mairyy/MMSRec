Title: Legendary: A Marvel Deck Building Game: Fantastic Four Expansion ; Description: The Fantastic Four and the Silver Surfer must unite to stop Galactus and his Heralds from destroying everything we know! Legendary: Fantastic Four is an expansion set to the highly acclaimed Legendary deck building card game. This 100 card expansion that will consist of new Heroes, Villains, Masterminds and Schemes in a scenario type model to expand even further on the already high level of variation and replay ability of the Legendary core set. (Legendary Fantastic Four is an expansion set not a standalone product and requires the Legendary core set in order to play). ;
