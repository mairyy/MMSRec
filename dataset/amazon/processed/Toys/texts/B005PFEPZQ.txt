Title: Go Ape! Card Game ; Description: This game adds a hilarious twist to the traditional game of Go Fish. Instead of asking other players for a card, you'll act out the funny monkey picture on the card. Collect the most monkey matches and you're the winner. If you like making funny faces and acting like a monkey, you'll go bananas over the Go Ape. card game. For 2 to 6 players. ;
