Title: Schleich Quetzalcoatlus ; Description: Schleich's Quetzalcoatlus is the perfect size for playing, Quetzalcoatlus named after an Aztec deity was the largest animal capable of flight ever found. With its impressive wingspan of over 13 metres this reptile also had a long neck and head and legs measuring almost three metres in length. Covered in a light fur this reptile was built like a bird equipped with hollow bones and sturdy wings for flight with the ability to skim waters for food. Despite its size Quetzalcoatlus probably only weighed a few 100 pounds. ;
