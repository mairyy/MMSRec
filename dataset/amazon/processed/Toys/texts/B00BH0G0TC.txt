Title: Chuggington StackTrack Brewster's Big City Adventure ; Description: Action Chugger set with Stack Track set lets you take the role of Action Chugger as he rescues Brewster. Brewster drives around the big city of Chuggington picking up trash and delivers it to the Recycling bin. On his way to the recycling bin he goes too fast and gets trapped in some overflowing trash. Action Chugger flies down with lights and sounds from the rooftops zooming down the ramp, dropping down the trick bridge and knocking Brewster free. All interaction points are modular to the track system allowing a variety of play configurations. ;
