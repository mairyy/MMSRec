Title: Brio Signal Station ; Description: Founded in Sweden in 1884, Brio makes the highest quality wooden toys that have delighted children and created happy memories for generations. Both educational and fun, Brio's uncompromising focus on design, quality and craftsmanship create toys of lasting value that stimulate creativity and imagination. You can control where the trains go with the Brio Signal Station. Turn the bright red knob to slide the switch plate to direct the train to the correct track as they fly by. Turning the knob also changes the red and green signals to alert the train engineer. The set comes with a poseable Brio figure, complete in yellow safety vest, who can fit into the Signal Station control room.
