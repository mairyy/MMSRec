Title: Carcassonne: Inns and Cathedrals ; Description: The expansion offers all fans of Carcassonne new challenges. The Cathedral triples the value of a city and the Inn doubles the value of a road but only when they are completed. Players may use the new large follower to double their chances of controlling a city, road, or field. Also included is all necessary material to allow a sixth player to join in the fun. Finally, there are six point tiles to make scoring easier when players score more than 50 points. You need to have the Carcassonne game in order to use this expansion. ;
