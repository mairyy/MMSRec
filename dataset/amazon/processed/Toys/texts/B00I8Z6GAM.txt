Title: Jumping Jack Game ; Description: Jumping Jack, catch him and win! This super cool bunny has planted a carrot garden around his hill. The only way to get him to jump off the hill is to pick the right carrot. The youngest player starts by flicking the spinner to see how many carrots to pull from Jack's bunny hill. Play will continue until pulling out the carrot that makes Jack jump. Catch him to win! Contents: 1 bunny hill, 1 Jumping Jack rabbit, 12 carrots, 1 spinner, 4 baskets and 1 instruction sheet. Ages 4 and up. 2 to 4 players. ;
