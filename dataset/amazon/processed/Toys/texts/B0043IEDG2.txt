Title: Snipe Game ; Description: The snipe hunt game can be played with two people, two teams or single seeker play and incorporates both indoor and outdoor activity and good old fashioned fun. The object of snipe is to be the first team/person to find the other team/persons Snipe and return it to it's nest. After the players have hidden their Snipe, the other player will try to find it. After a period of time, if the Snipes have not been discovered, they will chirp and then their eyes will light up. Each game comes with (2) snipes and a snipe nest. The packaging tells the story behind these legendary camp fire animals. ;
