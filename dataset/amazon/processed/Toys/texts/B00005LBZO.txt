Title: Kettler Kettcar Kabrio Cart ; Description: Built low to the ground like a go-cart, the Kettcar Kabrio is the ideal vehicle for the pintsize  racing fan in your family. Kids will enjoy the Kabrio's bright colors and comfortable ride, and parents will  appreciate the vehicle's sturdy construction. Designed for a smooth and safe ride on its four vinyl tread  wheels, this stylish ride-on vehicle features durable plastic components mounted on a strong tubular steel  frame. Measuring 37 inches long, the Kabrio comes with an adjustable high-back seat and a pretend  gearshift mounted on the centerboard. A brake lever mounted on the side of the seat can be pulled to slow  the vehicle or cause it to spin out. Adult supervision is recommended for younger children. Adult assembly required.--Chris Burns ;
