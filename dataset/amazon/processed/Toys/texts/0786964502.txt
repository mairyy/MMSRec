Title: Lords of Waterdeep: Scoundrels of Skullport Expansion Board Game ; Description: Scoundrels of Skullportadds TWO new expansions to theLords of Waterdeepboard game --UndermountainandSkullport-- inspired by the vast dungeon and criminal haven under Waterdeep. Players can choose to include one or both expansions in aLords of Waterdeepgame. The expansions also allow the addition of a sixth player.The Skullport expansion adds a new resource to the game: Corruption. The Undermountain expansion features bigger quests and more ways to get adventures.Scoundrels of Skullportalso includes new Lords, new Buildings, and set-up materials for a sixth player. ;
