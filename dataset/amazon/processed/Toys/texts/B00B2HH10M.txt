Title: Disney Mickey Mouse: Fly N Slide Clubhouse ; Description: The Mickey Mouse Clubhouse is where you find all your clubhouse pals right from the show. Clubhouse comes with two 2.75" figures (Mickey and Minnie). Door really opens and leads you into the clubhouse. Push down on the purple platform to drop your character down the chute. Your character will then reappear by passing through the toodles door and landing on the green slide platform. Go down the slide and play on the see-saw. Hop into the plane and ride around the Clubhouse. The handle on the top allows you bring the Clubhouse fun wherever you go. Set comes with Mickey, Minnie, see-saw, telescope, table and two stools. ;
