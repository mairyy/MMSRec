Title: VTech - V.Smile Rock &amp; Sing Musical Car ; Description: Strike up the band and watch as baby takes the lead on the piano. The Rock & Sing Musical Car teaches instrument sounds and musical notes and allows young music makers to create their own songs every time they play. Three sculpted musicians provide back up on the drums, guitar and saxophone, while five brightly colored piano keys respond to baby's touch with notes of their own. Three light-up buttons add to the learning fun with lessons in instrument sounds and a bright yellow handle makes it easy to push or pull the car when baby's on the go! ;
