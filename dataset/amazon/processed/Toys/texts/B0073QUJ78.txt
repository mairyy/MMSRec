Title: Schleich Brachiosaurus ; Description: Schleich's Brachiosaurus is the perfect size for playing, It was over 23 metres long and over six metres tall, like a giraffe, this dinosaur grazed on treetops. With chisel-like teeth and a very sharp sense of smell, this plant-eater always found enough to eat. Unlike most another dinosaurs, the Brachiosaurus' front legs were longer than its hind legs which made it easier for this herbivore to reach its food.
