Title: Hello Sunshine Game ; Description: For the first time, hide and seek and plush toys, two toddler favorites are brightly matched in an active play game. Parents and toddlers take turns hiding and finding Sunshine, a soft, huggable sun-shaped toy. As they excitedly seek Sunshine or hide it with glee toddlers learn to understand and respond to positional concepts such as Next To, Behind, On Top of and Under. An adorable first game for parents and toddlers to play together. ;
