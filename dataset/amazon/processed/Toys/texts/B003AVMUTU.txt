Title: Brio Travel Train ; Description: Founded in Sweden in 1884, Brio makes the highest quality wooden toys that have delighted children and created happy memories for generations. Both educational and fun, Brio's uncompromising focus on design, quality and craftsmanship create toys of lasting value that stimulate creativity and imagination. All aboard the red Brio Travel Train. It's the perfect train for any Brio railway. The train has a front, middle and rear train cars, an engineer and a passenger. Open the hinged cab and the engineer fits in securely. Open the hinged top of the passenger car and the Brio figure fits right in. This train trip's destination is limited only by your imagination. The Travel Train, as with everything in the Brio system, is engineered to work together for endless expansion and creative play options. ;
