Title: Z-Man Games Agricola: Farmers of the Moor ; Description: Agricola: Farmers of the Moor is a full-blown expansion to Agricola. It includes 150 cards, horse-shaped wooden pieces, many tiles, and a new scorepad. The mechanics include the need to heat your home during harvest as well as feeding your family; hence, fuel becomes a new resource to collect.  Farmers of the Moor includes special actions and lots of new major improvements. It expands the options to your Agricola experience. ;
