Title: Bruder Ausa Minidumper ; Description: This new BRUDER model is modeled on the Dumper D 350 AHG made by the Spanish firm AUSA. Just as its real-life brother, the BRUDER dumper is equipped with articulated joint steering and a tipping trough that can be rotated and has great off-road capabilities like the original. The vehicle comes with a rear coupling which allows it to be combined with numerous towed vehicles out of the BRUDER product range. ;
