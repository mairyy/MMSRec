SmartTalk technology gives Tiny the ability to recognize and interact with other dinosaurs in the line. Press her button, and as she shares tons of fun dino data. Interactive chomping sounds are triggered in Tiny's mouth, big flapping sounds are initiated by waving her wings: and when Tiny walks on the ground, she makes stomping sounds.
