Title: VTech Jake and the Neverland Pirates Spy and Learn Telescope ; Description: The Jake and the Never Land Pirates Spy and Learn Telescope is an interactive educational toy that lets your child play in three different ways. With a simple flip of the LCD screen, your child can switch from playing learning games on the outside of the telescope to playing learning games while gazing inside. For more imaginative play, your child can peer into the telescope like a real pirate to explore the world around them. With the push of a button, your child can even hear pirate rock songs for added fun. ;
