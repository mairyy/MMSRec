Title: Animal Upon Animal Stacking Game ; Description: Stack up the fun with Animal Upon Animal from HABA! This game is a winner of the Major Fun! Award and recipient of the Mr. Dad Seal of Approval. Players race each other to be the first to place all of their animals upon the animal pile before any of the other players. A roll of the die tells the players how many animals can be placed or whether they can add to the alligator base. This games fosters hand-eye coordination and fine motor skills. ;
