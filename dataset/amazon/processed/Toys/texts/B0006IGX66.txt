Title: DaVinci Challenge Game ; Description: This 2005 entry was the winner of an unprecedented 11 awards including the MENSA Select and the Oppenheim Platinum  awards. The game board consists of a pattern of circles called the Flower of Life. This Flower of Life symbol was first inscribed on pyramid walls over 6,000 years ago. Take turns placing shapes on the board to form nine different patterns. The more complex the pattern that you make, the more points you score. While very easy to learn, it is difficult to master.  DaVinci's Challenge is a 2 player (or two team) game, for ages 7 and up. ;
