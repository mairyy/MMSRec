Title: Chuggington Wooden Railway Zephie ; Description: Zephie is a young scissor-lift trolley, big enough to carry two people to work sites or destinations that need special attention. Unlike the other chuggers, she can spin around and scissor up and down in excitement.  She is flighty, giggly, girly and fun. Chuggington is her playground and she is always fascinated and excited about the wonders found there.  Chuggington Wooden Railway combines the charm of wooden train play with the exciting world of Chuggington! Easy-to-connect magnets are simple to connect to other vehicles.
