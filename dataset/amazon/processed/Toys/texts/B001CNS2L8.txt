Title: Say Anything ; Description: From the makers of the award-winning Wits and Wagers comes the next great party game - *Say Anything*. Say Anything is a light-hearted game about what you and your friends think. It gives you the chance to settle questions that have been hotly debated for centuries. For instance, &#x201C;What&#x2019;s the best movie of all time?&#x201D; or &#x201C;If I could have a BIG anything, what would it be?&#x201D; So dig deep into your heart or just come up with something witty &#x2013; this is your chance to Say Anything.   Say Anything combines the best elements of Wits and Wagers and Apples to Apples.   1) Ask any question from the card you draw, such as &#x201C;What&#x2019;s the best movie of all time?&#x201D; or &#x201C;If I could have a BIG anything, what would it be?&#x201D;   2) Everyone else writes an answer and turns it face-up.   3) Secretly choose your favorite of these answers. Then everyone tries to guess which one you picked.   Play *Say Anything* - and find out what your friends really think.   Ages: teens and adults. Players: 3 &#x2013; 8. Teach time: 2 minutes.
