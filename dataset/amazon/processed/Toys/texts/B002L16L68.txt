Title: Yikerz ; Description: Place your Yikerz pieces (magnets) down on the board and try to avoid attracting the other pieces already played. The object is to get rid of all your pieces. But beware, that play could backfire on your next turn. It includes convenient travel pouch for portable fun- anywhere, anytime! One of the reasons Yikerz is great game is because the unique pieces interchangeable game board designs allow for many different configurations that create variety of challenges and suits different skill levels.
