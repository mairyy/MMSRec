Title: Thomas &amp; Friends Make-A-Match Game ; Description: Thomas & Friends Make A Match Game: Preschool kids will love matching up their favorite Thomas & Friends characters. Begin by spreading the cards out face-down. Turn one card over so all can see. Then flip over a second card to reveal another picture. If they match, remove the cards and take another turn. If they don't match, turn the cards back over and the next player takes a turn. The player with the most matches wins. For 1 to 4 players. ;
