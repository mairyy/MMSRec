Title: Ravensburger Penguin Pile - Up - Children's Game ; Description: Ravensburger Penguin Pile-Up is a balancing act. Take one of your penguins and put it on the iceberg. The more penguins, the trickier it gets. You must take any penguins that fall off on your turn. The first to put all his penguins on the iceberg wins. You will learn cause and effect and fine motor skills. ;
