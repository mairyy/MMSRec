Title: Beyblades #BB119 JAPANESE Metal Fusion Death Quetzalcoatl 125RDF 4D ; Description: Death Quetzalcoatl 125RDF is a Balance-Type Beyblade that appears in the anime and manga series, Beyblade: Metal Fury. It is the 4D variation of Quetzalcoatl 90WF. It represents the planet Venus and is owned by Tithi. Death Quetzalcoatl's main feature is its Performance Tip: Rubber Defense Flat (RDF). RDF is unique in that it combines Attack power with Defense and Stamina aptitude, somewhat similar to CS and RSF. With this, it gives Death Quetzalcoatl a balance between the three main types in Beyblade. ;
