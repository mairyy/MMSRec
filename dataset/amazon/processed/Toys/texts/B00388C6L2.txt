Title: Thomas the Train: Zip, Zoom, and Logging Adventure ; Description: The Thomas and Friends Zip, Zoom and Logging Adventure train set is a motorized train set based on the Misty Island Rescue movie starring Thomas the Tank Engine and his merry friends. Create your own adventures on the island with Thomas as he meanders around the tracks, visits the mill to split a log, zooms across the zip-line bridge, and chugs through a tree trunk tunnel. This engaging train set is intended for children three years of age and older and requires adult assembly. ;
