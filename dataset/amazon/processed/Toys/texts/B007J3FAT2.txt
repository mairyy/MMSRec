Title: Imaginext Disney's Toy Story Pizza Planet Truck ; Description: The New Toy Story Pizza Planet Truck Brings a classic Toy Story vehicle into the product line. It features an activation disc on the roof that fires three included pizza projectiles out the grill. The projectiles are loaded by lifting up the hood. Also included are a Woody Figure and a Toolbox. As a cool design element, the back windows are labeled so it looks like the bed of the truck is full of aliens. ;
