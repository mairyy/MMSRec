Title: Schleich Spinosaurus ; Description: Schleich's Spinosaurus is the perfect size for playing, This deadly carnivore weighed several tons and could grow to be up to 17 metres long. The Spinosaurus had multiple sharp, straight teeth in its crocodile like mouth. Its meals mainly consisted of other dinosaurs or fish. The intelligently designed sail on its back served several functions. In cold blooded dinosaurs, the sail probably contributed to vital temperature regulation by collecting or distributing heat.
