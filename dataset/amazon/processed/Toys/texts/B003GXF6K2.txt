Title: Pictionary Card Game ; Description: Pictionary Card Game: Pictionary meets charades in the craziest card game ever, with no drawing required. Race to act out clues for your team using nothing but the simple images on each Picture Card, combine them, build scenes with them or use them as props. The first team to guess the correct answer in head-to-head play wins a point, and the first team to score 5 points wins the game. For 2 teams. ;
